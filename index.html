About Us

MASHRIQ HEIGHTS

Mashriq Heights is a thoughtfully crafted residential project developed by ConceptInfra, a professionally driven real estate and construction development firm built on engineering integrity, transparency, and long-term value creation.

Designed for discerning homeowners and global investors, the project reflects a commitment to refined living supported by strong technical foundations. ConceptInfra carries forward a strong local legacy, having served the city from the past decade under the name Concept Homes Construction, during which it has successfully planned and constructed numerous luxury residences.

This long-standing presence brings deep understanding of local conditions, execution discipline, and proven on-ground reliability. The company operates under the technical leadership of Dr. M. Yasir Khan, Doctorate in Civil Engineering from Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), whose professional journey is deeply connected with the academic heritage of the city.

For NRIs, professionals, and members of the AMU fraternity, Mashriq Heights offers clarity and confidence in an increasingly complex real estate environment. Every home is created with a level of detailing, construction quality, and modern planning standards that leave no room for compromise — setting a benchmark rather than following one.

Our quality promise is simple and unwavering: every residence is delivered exactly as envisioned, with no dilution in materials, workmanship, or living standards. Balanced by contemporary aesthetics, structural strength, and sustainable building principles, Mashriq Heights is designed to inspire trust today and preserve value for generations to come.
